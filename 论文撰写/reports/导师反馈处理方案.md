## 二、卡诺电池作为第二创新点的逻辑闭环

### 2.1 当前问题

现在论文中卡诺电池的定位比较弱——只是"集成了一个新设备"，没有形成闭环。

### 2.2 逻辑闭环设计

**核心论点**：卡诺电池是㶲加权匹配度方法的"试金石"——只有能区分能源品质的匹配度指标，才能正确识别电热耦合储能的价值。

**闭环逻辑**：

```
创新点1（理论）：㶲加权匹配度指标
    ↓ 理论预测
"能区分电热品质差异的指标，应该更善于引导电热耦合设备的配置"
    ↓ 实验验证
创新点2（应用）：卡诺电池集成
    ↓ 实验结果
"EQD方法配置的卡诺电池容量 > Std方法"（因为EQD能识别耦合价值）
    ↓ 反向验证
"卡诺电池实验反过来证明了㶲加权方法的优越性"
```

**具体论证**：

1. **正向论证**：㶲加权方法（λ_e=1.0, λ_h=0.131）天然地赋予电能更高权重。卡诺电池充电时消耗电能、产生余热（电→热），放电时消耗热能、产生电能（热→电）。㶲加权方法能准确评估这种跨品位能量转换的价值。

2. **反向验证**：如果Std方法（等权重λ=[1,1,1]）也能同样好地配置卡诺电池，那说明㶲加权没有额外价值。但实验结果表明EQD配置的卡诺电池容量更大、运行指标改善更明显——这反过来证明了㶲加权的必要性。

3. **闭环总结**：㶲加权方法不仅在传统设备配置上优于等权重方法（创新点1的验证），在新型耦合储能设备的配置上也展现出优势（创新点2的验证），两个创新点相互支撑。

## 四、Introduction修改方案

### 4.1 第4段（㶲分析文献综述）— 需要大改

**现有文本**（第7行）：
```
From a thermodynamic standpoint, different energy forms possess fundamentally
different qualities... The concept of energy quality coefficient (EQC)...

```

**修改为**：
```latex
From a thermodynamic standpoint, different energy forms possess fundamentally
different qualities, or abilities to perform useful work. Electricity, as pure
exergy, represents the highest quality energy carrier, while low-temperature
heating and cooling have significantly lower exergy content---typically only
10--15\% of the work potential of an equivalent amount of electrical energy
\cite{dincer2001energy,bejan2016advanced,【顶刊文献1】,【顶刊文献2】}.
The concept of energy quality coefficient (EQC), defined as the ratio of exergy
to energy, provides a rigorous framework for quantifying these differences
\cite{chen2019exergy,chen2022energy}.

In the context of IES optimization, exergy-based analysis has gained increasing
attention. Jiang et al. \cite{jiang2024joint} proposed a joint solution framework
for the optimal operation and evaluation of integrated energy systems based on
exergy, demonstrating that exergy analysis provides a unified scale for evaluating
both the quality and quantity of energy. The same group further developed an exergy
analysis model for integrated heat-and-electricity systems that minimizes total
exergy loss \cite{jiang2024exergy}. [Yang等的㶲-碳耦合优化] proposed an
exergy-carbon coupled optimization method for multi-energy systems. These studies
have established the value of exergy analysis in IES operational optimization and
evaluation. However, the application of exergy analysis to \emph{capacity planning}
---specifically, using Carnot exergy factors as energy quality weights in source-load
matching quantification---remains unexplored. Ignoring energy quality differences
in matching assessment can lead to suboptimal planning decisions: equal weighting
implicitly treats 1~kW of electrical mismatch as equivalent to 1~kW of thermal
mismatch---a premise that contradicts the second law of thermodynamics
\cite{moran2014fundamentals}---and may cause the optimizer to over-invest in
low-grade thermal storage when the same investment in electrical storage would
yield greater overall system benefit.
```

### 4.2 贡献段（第6段）— 需要重写

**修改为**：
```latex
To address these research gaps, this paper makes two mutually reinforcing
contributions:

First, an energy-quality-weighted three-dimensional Euclidean distance index
is proposed for quantifying source-load matching in CCHP systems. Unlike
previous studies that adopt empirically adjusted energy quality coefficients,
and distinct from existing exergy-based approaches that focus on operational
optimization and evaluation \cite{jiang2024joint,jiang2024exergy}, this work
applies Carnot exergy factors to a fundamentally different problem: weighting
the relative importance of different energy carriers in a planning-stage
matching index. The resulting coefficients are parameter-free, uniquely
determined by supply temperatures and ambient conditions. Post-optimization
operational analysis confirms that the Carnot-factor-weighted index produces
solutions with superior grid-interaction characteristics---including 26.7\%
lower peak grid demand and 47.5\% higher self-sufficiency rate compared to
the conventional volatility-based method at equivalent budget levels.

Second, a Carnot battery model with waste heat recovery is integrated into
the CCHP system topology as a representative electro-thermal coupled storage
device, and its capacity is co-optimized within the bi-objective framework.
The Carnot battery's inherent cross-carrier coupling---converting electricity
to heat during charging and recovering electricity from heat during
discharging---serves as a natural testbed for evaluating the proposed
energy-quality-weighted index. Numerical experiments demonstrate that the
proposed index more effectively guides Carnot battery sizing than the
equal-weight alternative, confirming that energy quality differentiation
is essential for optimizing coupled storage technologies. The two contributions
thus form a closed validation loop: the theoretical innovation enables
effective integration of the technological innovation, and the technological
innovation reciprocally validates the theoretical foundation.
```
